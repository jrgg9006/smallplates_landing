SMALL PLATES — CREATIVE SYSTEM v1
The emotional anthropology of modern relationships
1. CORE IDEA

Small Plates is not a wedding brand.

Small Plates is:

the emotional anthropology of modern relationships.

We observe, capture, and express what it feels like to:

fall in love
get married
build a life
stay connected

In a world that makes all of that harder than it should be.

2. THE EMOTIONAL TRUTH

This is the engine behind everything we create.

Modern relationships are:

beautiful
absurd
overwhelming
quiet
deeply meaningful

People today:

are hyper-connected digitally
but emotionally disconnected
crave real moments
struggle to maintain them
Core truths:
Modern love is absurd
People are trying their best
Connection requires effort
Adulthood is confusing
Small moments matter most
People you love shouldn’t disappear

INSTEAD expressed through:
