# Texture

Small Plates is tactile.

If it does not look touchable,
it does not belong.

---

## We Favor:

- ceramic
- linen
- paper
- wood
- fingerprints
- scratches
- patina

---

## We Reject:

❌ plastic  
❌ gloss  
❌ high-shine  
❌ digital flatness  
❌ synthetic finishes  

Every texture must feel:
used.
written on.
lived with.
