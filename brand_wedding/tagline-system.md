# Tagline System

*Two lines. One purpose each. Never reversed.*

---

## The Two Lines

| Line Type | Tagline | Function |
|-----------|---------|----------|
| **Brand Line** | *"Still at the table."* | Emotional resonance, identity, closing moments |
| **Acquisition Line** | *"Recipes from the people who love you."* | Functional clarity, attraction, opening moments |

Both lines are defined as canonical in `brand_idea.md` Four Articulations. This doc is the usage system.

---

## The Rule

**Acquisition Line:** opens the door.
**Brand Line:** closes it.

The functional line earns attention by creating understanding. The brand line earns power by coming after understanding. Never reverse them.

**No permitted variations.** Both lines run exact. Drift dilutes.

## The Journey — How the Lines Land

### Couple-facing (default funnel)

1. **Discovery (ad):** *"Recipes from the people who love you."*
2. **Consideration (landing):** Hero = acquisition line. Emotional section below = *"Ten years from now... they're still at the table."*
3. **Purchase (confirmation email):** *"Your book is in motion. Soon, everyone who loves [Bride] will be in one place. Still at the table."*
4. **Delivery (inside book):** Last page = *"Still at the table."*

### Organizer-facing

1. **Discovery (ad):** *"A wedding gift made by everyone. Managed by no one."*
2. **Consideration (organizer landing):** Hero = *"Meaningful gift. Zero management overhead."* · How-it-works = *"You set it up. We handle the rest."*
3. **Setup confirmation:** *"Your book is live. Invitations go out today. Reminders are automated."*
4. **Progress email:** *"28 of 42 recipes in. Reminder #2 goes out tomorrow. Everything's on track."*
5. **Completion:** *"The book is done. It's on its way. You did this — without losing your mind."*
