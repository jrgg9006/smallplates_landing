# Composition

Small Plates composes like memory.

Nothing is perfectly centered emotionally.

---

## We Favor:

✅ asymmetry  
✅ negative space  
✅ breathing margins  
✅ incomplete scenes  
✅ partial frames  

---

## We Reject:

❌ symmetry for beauty’s sake  
❌ product grids  
❌ clutter  
❌ digital rhythm  

---

## Spatial Feeling

Images should feel:

- entered
- stepped into
- remembered

Not presented.

If the image feels flat,
it’s wrong.

It should feel like:
you walked into it.
