## How Customers Arrive Today

### Channel 1: Word of Mouth
- Someone sees or hears about Small Plates through personal network
- No tracking, no attribution, no funnel
- Estimated volume: 6 weddings to date. 100% word of mouth.

### Channel 2: Gifted Books
- We gift books to select couples at cost ($59)
- Goal: create product advocates who refer future organizers
- This IS our current acquisition strategy, intentionally
- No formal tracking of referral chain yet

### Channel 3: Ana Karen's Network
- Co-founder does all public-facing activity
- Instagram presence being built (2x/week posting)
- No paid amplification
