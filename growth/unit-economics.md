## The Gifted Book as CAC

Currently our acquisition strategy is gifting books to couples.
Full cost to us per gifted book:


| Item          | Cost (USD) |
| ------------- | ---------- |
| Printing      | $40.00     |
| Shipping      | $14.00     |
| Tech          | $5.00      |
| **Total CAC** | **$59.00** |


If one gifted book generates one paying organizer → 
CAC = $59, Gross Profit on first sale = $110. 
Payback: immediate. This only works if k ≥ 1.

## Upsell: Extra Copies

Price per extra copy: $119 USD (confirmed pricing)
COGS per extra copy: ~$54 USD (printing only; no additional shipping charge when sent to the same address, no tech overhead)
Gross profit per extra copy: ~$65 USD
Margin: ~55%

Each book that sells 1+ extra copies significantly improves 
the overall economics per wedding.
